Which program do I use the most? Why is software design important to me?

     Personally, I use Visual Studio Code on windows the most. I feel it is designed well because it supports most of the languages I use, such as HTML and Python. I also find the visual designs help me immensely in distinguishing data types and lines of code, which makes coding easier for me. After a couple weeks of this course, software design has opened my eyes on the importance of planning your code. Much like my personal experience in professional cooking, I see the development stage of a software akin to creating a recipe for great dish. It is important to ensure the product we are creating and delivering meets the needs of the specific demographic it is intended for. I particularly enjoy the concept of pausing to encourage thinking for thorough understanding when approaching a problem. I feel this can help with maintaining the integrity of a product rather than rushing to produce something for quick sales or for the sake of being a pioneer. In the age of a growing population and dwindling resources, I think we all should take a step back to pause and plan for a better future.
